FAQ

The questions we hear most.

Why a monthly fee?

Your site needs hosting, security updates, content edits, and monitoring. The monthly covers all of it — no surprise invoices, ever.

Can I cancel?

Yes, after a minimum 6-month commitment. After that, cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. You keep the site and domain.

Who owns the website?

You do. The domain is registered in your name from day one. If you ever leave, we hand over everything.

What if I already have a domain?

Perfect. We connect to your existing domain at no extra cost. If you don't have one, we help you pick and register it.

How fast is launch?

Starter: 7–10 days. Growth: 10–14 days. Pro: 14–21 days. Assuming timely feedback and content on your side.

How do I pay?

Setup 50% upfront, 50% on launch. Payments via Wise or Payoneer — invoice with instructions provided. Card payments coming soon.
