FAQ

Questions homeowners ask us most.

How much does a new roof cost in Austin?

Most residential asphalt roof replacements in Austin run between $8,000 and $18,000 depending on size and materials. We give you a firm written quote — no surprises.

Do you offer free inspections?

Yes — always free, no obligation. We inspect, document, and give you an honest assessment whether you need work or not.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential replacements are completed in 1–2 days. Larger homes or complex roofs may take 3. We leave your property cleaner than we found it.

Can you handle my insurance claim?

Absolutely. We meet your adjuster on-site, document everything, and advocate for your claim. We've handled over 800 insurance jobs.

What warranties do you offer?

Lifetime workmanship warranty from Apex, plus manufacturer warranties on materials (up to 50 years on premium shingles, lifetime on metal).

Do I need to be home during the install?

Nope. Most customers go to work while we do the job. We'll text you updates and photos throughout the day.
